February 13, 2015 - Amsterdam

NG-NL

The NG-NL Conference in Amsterdam is the first AngularJS Conference that will take place in The Netherlands. One full day we will be hosting sessions and workshops about AngularJS and related topics.

All sessions will be in English and the conference will be from 9am to 5pm (doors will open earlier for registration). Early bird tickets will go on sale the 6th of December! Please follow us on twitter at @ngnlconf to stay up to date.
